# Privacy Policy for Rupee Garden

**Last updated:** January 29, 2026

## Overview
Rupee Garden is a personal finance tracking app that helps users track daily savings and spending habits.

## Data Collection
Rupee Garden collects and stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Daily saving/spending entries
- Spending amounts and categories
- User progress (XP, level, streaks)
- Achievement unlock status
- Monthly budget settings

## Data Storage
- All data is stored **locally on your device**
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No cloud sync or backup services are used
- No account or login is required

## Data Sharing
- We do **NOT** share any data with third parties
- We do **NOT** collect analytics or usage data
- We do **NOT** use advertising SDKs
